Privacy Policy for Smodr

Last Updated: November 23, 2025

Introduction

Smodr ("we", "our", or "the app") is a podcast player application for Android devices. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Smodr collects minimal information to provide its core functionality:

  • Podcast Feed Data: The app downloads and stores podcast feed information (titles, descriptions, artwork, audio URLs) from RSS feeds you subscribe to.
  • Playback Information: The app stores your playback progress, completed episodes, and preferences locally on your device.
  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app. This may include app crashes, feature usage, and general device information.

How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Provide podcast playback functionality
  • Remember your preferences and playback progress
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand how users interact with the app to prioritize feature development

Data Storage and Security

  • All podcast subscriptions, playback history, and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Analytics data is transmitted securely to Firebase Analytics servers
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes

Third-Party Services

Smodr uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics: For anonymous usage analytics. See Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Podcast RSS Feeds: The app connects directly to podcast RSS feeds provided by podcast creators

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Uninstall the app at any time, which will remove all locally stored data
  • Clear app data through your device settings
  • Opt out of analytics by disabling them in your device settings

Children's Privacy

Smodr is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Open Source

Smodr is an open-source project. You can review the source code at https://github.com/cascadiacollections/smodr.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
